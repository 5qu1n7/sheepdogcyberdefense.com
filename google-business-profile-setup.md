# Google Business Profile Setup Guide

## Sheepdog Cyber Defense

This guide walks you through setting up your Google Business Profile for local SEO in the Bulverde, Spring Branch, and San Antonio area.

---

## Step 1: Create or Claim Your Business

1. Go to [business.google.com](https://business.google.com)
2. Click **Manage now** and sign in with your Google account
3. Enter your business name: **Sheepdog Cyber Defense**
4. Enter your business category: **Cybersecurity Service**

---

## Step 2: Enter Your Business Address

Since you're a home-based business in Bulverde:

1. Enter your physical address (or use a P.O. Box if preferred)
2. **Important**: Check the box that says "I deliver goods and services to my customers at their location"
3. Service areas: Bulverde, Spring Branch, San Antonio, Texas

This hides your exact address from the public while still showing you serve the area.

---

## Step 3: Contact Information

- **Phone**: Use your business phone number
- **Website**: https://sheepdogcyberdefense.com

---

## Step 4: Business Details

**Category**: Cybersecurity Service (primary)

**Attributes** (select all that apply):
- Veteran-owned business
- Women-owned: No (uncheck)
- LGBTQ+ owned: No (uncheck)
- Wheelchair accessible entrance: Only if applicable

**Service menu**: Add your 5 services:
1. External Penetration Testing
2. Internal Penetration Testing
3. Web Application Testing
4. Red Teaming
5. Physical Site Review

---

## Step 5: Add Photos

Upload these types of photos:

1. **Logo** - Your Sheepdog logo
2. **Cover photo** - Professional image representing your business
3. **Team photos** - If you have team members
4. **Office photos** - If you have a professional office space
5. **Working photo** - Action shot of consulting work

---

## Step 6: Verify Your Business

Google will send a **postcard** to your business address with a verification code (takes 5-14 days).

1. Go to your Business Profile
2. Click "Verify now"
3. Enter the code from the postcard

**Verification is required** before your listing appears in search results.

---

## Step 7: Optimize Your Profile

### Business Description

Write a description (750 characters max):

> Sheepdog Cyber Defense is a veteran-owned cybersecurity company serving Bulverde, Spring Branch, San Antonio, and South Central Texas. We provide penetration testing, red teaming, web application security testing, and physical security assessments. Protect your business with professional security testing from a trusted local partner.

### Posts

Create regular posts to stay active:
- Announce new blog posts
- Share security tips
- Promote seasonal offers

### Q&A

Add common questions and answers:

**Q: What areas do you serve?**
A: We serve Bulverde, Spring Branch, San Antonio, and all of South Central Texas.

**Q: What industries do you work with?**
A: We work with small to medium businesses across all industries, including healthcare, finance, retail, and manufacturing.

---

## Local SEO Tips

1. **Use your Google Business Profile consistently** across all directories
2. **Respond to all reviews** (request reviews from happy clients)
3. **Post regularly** (at least once per week)
4. **Answer questions** in the Q&A section
5. **Keep information updated** - especially hours and phone number

---

## Important Notes

- You need a physical address to verify (even if you work from home)
- Hide your address in settings if concerned about privacy
- Verification postcard cannot be expedited
- Once verified, you can manage from the Google Maps app

---

1. Wait for verification postcard (5-14 days)
2. Complete all profile sections
3. Add photos
4. Request reviews from clients
5. Post updates weekly
