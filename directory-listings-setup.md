# Directory Listings Setup Guide

## Sheepdog Cyber Defense

This guide covers setting up profiles on key B2B directories for cybersecurity companies.

---

## Clutch.co

### Overview
Clutch is a B2B ratings and reviews platform. Clients search here to find and evaluate service providers.

### How to Get Listed

1. **Claim or Create Profile**
   - Go to [clutch.co/profile/sheepdog-cyber-defense](https://clutch.co/profile/sheepdog-cyber-defense) (search for your company)
   - If not found, click "Claim Profile" or contact their team

2. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Business name: Sheepdog Cyber Defense
   - Website: https://sheepdogcyberdefense.com
   - Phone: Your business number
   - Email: sheepdogcyberdefense@protonmail.com
   - Location: Bulverde, TX
   - Year founded: 2022

3. **Select Service Lines**
   - IT Services
   - Cybersecurity
   - Penetration Testing
   - Security Consulting

4. **Add Certifications**
   - GWAPT
   - PNPT
   - PWPP
   - PWPA
   - CEH
   - CRTO
   - eCPPTv2

5. **Add Portfolio Items**
   - Include anonymized case studies
   - Focus on industries served
   - Highlight results achieved

6. **Request Reviews**
   - Ask satisfied clients to leave reviews on Clutch
   - Reviews significantly impact rankings

### Profile Completion Tips
- Write a detailed "About Us" (500+ words)
- Add team members with photos and bios
- Include case studies with metrics
- Keep information updated

## UpCity

### Overview
UpCity is another B2B marketplace connecting businesses with service providers.

### How to Get Listed

1. **Create Account**
   - Go to [upcity.com](https://upcity.com)
   - Click "For Businesses" or search for your company

2. **Claim Profile**
   - Search for "Sheepdog Cyber Defense"
   - Claim or create your business profile

3. **Complete Profile Sections**
   - Business information
   - Services offered
   - Service areas (Texas, Bulverde, San Antonio)
   - Certifications
   - Contact information
   - Website link

4. **Get Verified**
   - UpCity may verify your business
   - Complete verification steps

5. **Encourage Reviews**
   - Request reviews from clients
   - More reviews = better visibility

## NAP Consistency (Critical)

**NAP** = Name, Address, Phone

Ensure your business information is **identical** across all directories:

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Name | Sheepdog Cyber Defense |
| Phone | [Your phone] |
| Address | [Your Bulverde address] |
| Website | https://sheepdogcyberdefense.com |

### Why This Matters
- Inconsistent NAP confuses search engines
- Can negatively impact local SEO rankings
- Google cross-references directory information

---

## Best Practices

1. **Claim all profiles** - Don't let others create incorrect listings
2. **Complete every field** - More complete = better visibility
3. **Stay consistent** - Use same name, phone, address everywhere
4. **Add photos** - Professional photos improve credibility
5. **Respond to reviews** - Both positive and negative
6. **Update regularly** - Keep information current
